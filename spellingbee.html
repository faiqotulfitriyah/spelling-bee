How to Play

Here's how it works:

  • Each turn, we'll play you a recording of a word. You try to guess the spelling.
  • You'll get 10 random questions from each level.
  • Only your first guess counts towards your score.
  • If you cannot answer it, you can click the "Hint" button for once.



Wear your headphones!

Hearing the subtleties in the pronunciations can really help improve your score, so we recommend that you wear headphones while playing.
